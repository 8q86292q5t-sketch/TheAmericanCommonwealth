The American Commonwealth

A working draft for a public-facing site about institutions, local power, political culture, and practical reforms.

Open draft Shape the next prompt
Draft spine

A serious civic publication with room to become a platform.

This first draft frames the site as a thoughtful publication and project hub. It can move toward essays, research notes, policy explainers, local chapter tooling, a newsletter, or a members area without changing the visual direction.

The tone is institutional without feeling frozen: archival, editorial, and practical.

Editorial lanes

Start with four durable content tracks.

Each lane is intentionally broad so future prompts can turn it into pages, feeds, databases, or interactive tools.

01

Institutions

Congress, courts, parties, states, cities, and the operating rules behind them.

02

Political Culture

Language, symbols, habits, media, and the norms that shape civic life.

03

Local Power

Municipal systems, school boards, zoning, budgets, public meetings, and local capacity.

04

Reform Workshop

Plain-language proposals, implementation notes, tradeoffs, and scorecards.
